The Last Call is Fast-Paced, VR, Service-game. Set in a fantasy setting, Take up the role of the "Potion Master", serving potions to Goblin and Dwarven patrons at the Gingers Ale. As the last call for potions rolls in, Can you serve them all before the night ends? 

Roles: General Programmer

Contributions: 
- Sourced and Implemented music and sound effects like the cauldron, enchantment table, and voice lines for Dwarf Characters
- Developed Gameplay mechanics such as the Bar taps that fill bottles with liquids
- Ensured Meta Quest 3 VR headset worked correctly and perfomed bug fixing
