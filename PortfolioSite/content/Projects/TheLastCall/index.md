3rd year Capstone Project

The Last Call is Fast-Paced, VR, Service-game. Set in a fantasy setting, Take up the role of the "Potion Master", serving potions to your Goblin and Dwarven patrons at the Gingers Ale. As the last call for potions rolls in, Can you serve them all before the night ends?
