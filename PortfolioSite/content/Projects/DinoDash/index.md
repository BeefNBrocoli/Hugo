2nd Year Rappid Game Development Project

Dino Dash is a 2D Platformer where you play as Lil' Guy - a little red raptor trying to make his way back to his nest after being chased away from his home by a huge T-Rex. Navigate the world through platforming, dashing, and eating to keep to your energy up.
